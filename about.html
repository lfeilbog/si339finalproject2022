Tikkun Olam Makers (TOM)

Tikkun Olam Makers (TOM) is a global network of communities dedicated to improving people's lives by making affordable open-source technology accessible to all.

We connect local Makers with Need-Knowers (individuals with a personal understanding of unmet needs) to design and manufacture customized solutions.

We believe that every person should have access to affordable assistive technology and solutions that would improve their quality of life and allow them to participate fully in society on a daily basis.

Meaning Behind the Name

  • Tikkun Olam
    Refers to a Jewish concept calling upon every person to make a contribution toward improving the condition of their community and the world
  • Makers
    Refers to the Maker movement and the growing power of the "do-it-yourself revolution" to customize solutions through new technologies and distributed manufacturing
