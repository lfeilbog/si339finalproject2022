TOM Global Solutions

TOM's Global Database

All projects, in varying stages of development, are uploaded to the TOM Online Marketplace so they can be easily accessed and downloaded for production, customization, or further development by other makers or communities

Highlighted Products in Database

Gyro Cup

The Gyro Cup connects to crutches and stabilizes the user's cup to prevent it from spilling any liquid.

Key Holder

The Key Holder enables people who lack motor skills to open any key-using mechanism independently.

Dice Box

The TOM Dice Box helps people who lack fine motor skills play dice-based games. The product is made from a plastic dome, a 3D printed base, and two dice. The dome can be bought at any hardware store.

Violin Prosthesis

The PJ Prosthesis Soft Version is a flexible prosthesis base design that enables amputees to independently perform daily functions and hobbies that require precise natural movements.

Cuff Utensil Holder

The Cuff Utensil Holder helps people who have challenges with fine motor skills and hand stability eat independently.

Mini Wheelchair

An affordable wheelchair designed for young children to help enable more independence within the home environment.

Finger Brush

The Finger Brush device is designed to simplify the action of painting for people with limited hand or finger control.

Ring Book Holder

The Ring Book Holder extends the grip of the thumb and enable the user to hold a book with just one hand.

Toothbrush Holder

The Toothbrush Holder helps individuals who lack fine motor skills or hand stability hold a toothbrush.
